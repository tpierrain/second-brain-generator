# 2025-11-20 — Create the Inertia Trophy

> **Fictional** decision (parody company) in MADR-lite format, for the demo. A decision is
> **immutable**: if we change our minds, we create a new decision that supersedes this one.

## Context
At [[topics/flemmr]], the internal culture prizes unapologetic idleness. All that remained was to
mark it officially, to embody the brand for the whole roster.

## Decision
Establish the **Inertia Trophy**, an annual distinction that crowns the person posting the highest
**Do-Nothing Rate (DNR)** — the one who produced strictly nothing all year long.

## Consequences
- First crowning: the **2025 Inertia Trophy** goes to **Pélagie de Mollecuisse**, with a
  **record DNR of 98.7%** — a peak of unproductiveness never before reached in the house.
- Ceremony run by [[people/jean-kevin-de-la-glandee]].

Accepted.
